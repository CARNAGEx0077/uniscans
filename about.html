About Uniscans & Labs

A legacy of precision, trust, and advanced healthcare since 2010.

Excellence in Medical Diagnostics

Welcome to Uniscans & Labs. For over a decade, we have dedicated ourselves to transforming the landscape of medical diagnostics by combining state-of-the-art technological infrastructure with the expertise of seasoned medical professionals. We understand that a correct diagnosis is the crucial first step to effective treatment.

We pride ourselves on providing a patient-centric environment. Whether it's a routine blood test or an advanced MRI scan, every procedure is carried out with utmost care, minimal wait times, and a commitment to clinical excellence.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and accessible diagnostic services to all segments of society utilizing advanced medical technology and compassionate care.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted diagnostic partner, establishing a benchmark for global quality standards in pathology and radiology.

Our Commitment to Quality

We do not compromise on accuracy. Our infrastructure, reagents, and reporting protocols are heavily scrutinized to meet global standards.

NABL Accredited & ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Ensuring the highest global standards in diagnostic testing.
