Test Preparation Guides

Proper preparation ensures accurate results. Follow these guidelines for your upcoming test.

Blood Tests (Fasting Required)

Applies to: Lipid Profile, Fasting Blood Sugar, Liver Function, Kidney Function, Iron Studies

Before the Test

  • Fast for 10-12 hours before your appointment
  • Only plain water is allowed during fasting
  • Avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours before
  • Continue regular medications unless advised otherwise by your doctor
  • Get adequate sleep the night before

During the Test

  • A small amount of blood (2-5 ml) will be drawn from your arm
  • The process takes only 2-3 minutes
  • Inform the technician if you feel dizzy during blood draw

After the Test

  • You can eat and drink normally after the sample is collected
  • Keep the bandage on for 30 minutes
  • Results in 12-24 hours via email/SMS
Blood Tests (No Fasting)

Applies to: CBC, Thyroid (T3,T4,TSH), HbA1c, Vitamin D, Vitamin B12, CRP

  • No fasting required — eat and drink normally
  • Stay well hydrated before the test
  • Inform the technician about any medications you're taking
  • Results in 24-48 hours via email/SMS
MRI Scan Preparation

Duration: 20-45 minutes depending on body part

Before the Scan

  • Remove all metal objects: jewelry, watches, belts, coins, hair pins, body piercings
  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing without metal (zippers, buttons, underwired bras)
  • We provide a hospital gown if needed
  • Inform us about: pacemakers, metal implants, cochlear implants, aneurysm clips
  • If you're claustrophobic, inform us in advance (we can provide medication)
  • No fasting unless your doctor specifically requests contrast MRI

During the Scan

  • Lie still during the entire scan for clear images
  • You'll hear loud tapping/humming sounds (earplugs provided)
  • You can communicate with the technician through an intercom
  • Emergency buzzer provided if you feel uncomfortable

⚠️ Important: MRI is NOT safe for patients with pacemakers, certain metal implants, or metallic foreign objects. Please disclose all medical devices.

CT Scan Preparation

Duration: 10-15 minutes

  • With Contrast: Fast for 4-6 hours before. Drink plenty of water. Inform about kidney problems or allergies
  • Without Contrast: No fasting or special preparation needed
  • Wear comfortable clothing, remove metal objects from scan area
  • Inform about pregnancy or suspected pregnancy
  • Bring previous scan reports if available
Ultrasound Preparation

Abdominal Ultrasound

  • Fast for 6-8 hours before the scan
  • Only water is allowed
  • Full bladder may be required — drink 4-5 glasses of water 1 hour before

Pelvic/Pregnancy Ultrasound

  • Full bladder required
  • Drink 4-5 glasses of water 1 hour before and don't urinate
  • No fasting needed

Thyroid/Breast Ultrasound

  • No preparation required
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing
ECG / 2D Echo / TMT Preparation

ECG (Electrocardiogram)

  • No special preparation needed
  • Avoid lotions/creams on chest area
  • Wear comfortable, front-opening clothing
  • Duration: 5-10 minutes

2D Echo (Echocardiography)

  • No fasting required
  • Wear loose clothing
  • Duration: 15-20 minutes

TMT (Treadmill Test)

  • Light meal 2 hours before (avoid heavy food)
  • Wear running shoes and comfortable clothing
  • Avoid caffeine and smoking for 4 hours before
  • Bring your current medication list
  • Duration: 20-30 minutes
X-Ray Preparation
  • Generally no preparation needed
  • Remove metal objects from the area being X-rayed
  • Inform if you are or could be pregnant
  • Lead shield will be provided to protect other body parts
  • Duration: 5-10 minutes

These are general guidelines. Specific tests may have additional requirements. We'll send specific preparation instructions when you book.
