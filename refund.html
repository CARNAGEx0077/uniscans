We strive to provide a seamless booking experience. However, we understand that plans may change. Below is our complete refund and cancellation policy.

2. How to Cancel

  • Phone: Call +91 XXXXX-XXXXX with your booking ID
  • WhatsApp: Send your booking ID to +91 XXXXX-XXXXX
  • Email: cancellations@[DOMAIN] with booking ID and reason

You will receive a cancellation confirmation via SMS and email within 30 minutes.

3. Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refund will be credited to the original payment method
  • Cash payments: refund via bank transfer (provide bank details)
  • UPI/Card: automatic reversal to your account

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Sample already collected (blood drawn, urine collected)
  • Scan already performed (MRI/CT/X-Ray/Ultrasound begun)
  • Reports already generated and delivered
  • Home collection fee (if applicable) once phlebotomist dispatched

5. Rescheduling

You can reschedule your appointment free of charge up to 2 times per booking. After 2 reschedules, subsequent changes will be treated as cancellations and new bookings.

6. Re-testing Policy

If you believe your test result is incorrect:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving the report
  • We will offer free re-testing with a fresh sample
  • If the original result is confirmed, no additional charges apply

7. Force Majeure

In case of cancellations due to natural disasters, equipment failure, or circumstances beyond our control, a full refund or free rescheduling will be provided.
