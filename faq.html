Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers regarding tests, timings, and procedures.

How quickly will I get my blood test results?

Most standard blood tests and routine profiles are delivered within 12-24 hours via email and SMS. Specialized tests might take 48-72 hours depending on the complexity of the culture.

Do I need to book an appointment for an X-ray?

Walk-ins are welcome for general X-rays and routine blood tests. However, for specialized imaging like MRI, CT Scans, or detailed ultrasounds, a prior appointment is highly recommended to avoid wait times.

Do you offer home sample collection?

Yes, we offer free home sample collection for tests billing over a certain amount within a 15km radius of our diagnostic center. Our trained phlebotomists follow strict hygiene protocols.

Is Uniscans & Labs NABL accredited?

Absolutely. We are fully NABL accredited and ISO certified, which ensures that our lab protocols, equipment calibration, and final reports meet the highest international medical standards.
