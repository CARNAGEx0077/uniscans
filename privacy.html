Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [DATE]

At Uniscans & Labs ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and medical information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full name, date of birth, age, and gender
  • Contact details: phone number, email address, home address
  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN) for identity verification
  • Emergency contact details

Health & Medical Information

  • Doctor prescriptions and referrals
  • Medical history, current medications, and allergies
  • Diagnostic test results and reports
  • Imaging scans (MRI, CT, X-Ray, Ultrasound images)

Technical Information

  • Browser type, device information, IP address
  • Cookies and usage analytics (see Section 6)
  • Booking history and interaction data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process bookings and conduct diagnostic tests
  • To generate, store, and deliver test reports
  • To communicate appointment reminders and health alerts
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • For internal quality control and audit purposes

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal or medical information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Your referring doctor — with your explicit consent
  • Insurance providers — for claim processing with your authorization
  • Regulatory authorities — as required by law (e.g., notifiable diseases)
  • Technology partners — who assist in report delivery (under strict NDAs)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with restricted access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection protocols
  • Anonymization of data used for research/analytics

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and medical data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login sessions
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Improve website functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.
